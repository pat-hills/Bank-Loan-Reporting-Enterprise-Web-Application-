What Repurta Does?

Repurta provides an insightful variety of business metrics set by financial institutions for businesses into one condensed report form to be submitted every month.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have a question to be answered? Is time to address them all. Dont forget to submit any question or concern to our contact form.

  • What is Repurta?

    Repurta is a data analytics reporting and evaluation platform that can be used to assess past, present, and future business information while keeping track of the overall performance of a business together with its creditworthiness and all monitoring processes to help financial institutions in decision making before and after giving out loans

  • How does Repurta help businesses?

    Repurta fasttrack all loan and applications to financial institutions and easy smarter of reporting progress reports of their businesses

  • Can I use Repurta as my own reporting platform?

    No. Repurta, was built for financial institutions to monitor businesses who apply loans and to report progress updates of their business. However businesses can always export their data or share with other stakeholders should the need arises

  • How can submit my monthly report?

    As a business you need to apply for a loan on the platform and wait for approval. After approval you would automatically see all the reporting metrics set by your financial institution

  • How can financial institutions set up and how easy is the process?

    Financial institutions can always request for a demo by contacting Bsystems. The Setup process is very easy and simple. After the set up the portfolio of the institution would be on the platform and based on the proximity business can always apply for loans.
