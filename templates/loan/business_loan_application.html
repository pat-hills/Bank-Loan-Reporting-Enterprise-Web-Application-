Smart E-Business Loan Application

Understanding the requirements in the application is as important as providing the right information in the form fields. Below are certain tips for completing your business loan application form.

1. Application letter (pdf) upload stating;
  • the type of facility
  • amount required
  • the purpose
  • the period for which the facility is required
    • 2. Company's Resolution letter upload (pdf) on company's letterhead (according to GCB format attached) from the Board of Directors authorising the borrowing.
    3. Shareholder's Resolution letter upload (pdf) on company's letterhead (according to GCB format attached) if amount required is more than the stated capital of the organization.
    4. Audited financial statement (Income Statement and Balance sheet) upload (excel) for the past 3 years (or statement of State of Affairs upload (pdf) if company is new)
    5. Latest Management accounts (Balance sheet and Profit and Loss) for the current year upload (excel)
    6. Projected Cash flow statement upload (excel) covering the period for which the facility is required
    7. Valuation Report of Property upload (pdf) to be used to secure the facility
    8. Copies of title Deeds of propert(ies) upload (pdf) that will used as security to support the application
    9. Execution of statutory declaration upload (pdf) if property is not owned by the company
    10. A search report upload from the Lands Commission on the property addressed to the head of Legal GBC Bank

    11. Evidence of Property Rate Paid upload

    12. Ground Rent Paid upload (For leasehold properties)

    14. Details of members of Board of Directors (Name, Position, Age, Nationality) and CV upload
    15. Ownership structure (Name, Percentage Shareholding)
    16. Details Of relationships with other Banks and financial institutions (Name of bank, type of relationships,period)
